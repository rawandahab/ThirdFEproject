Popular destinations in Egypt

Major tourist destinations include the millennia-old monuments in the Nile Valley. Principle among them are the Pyramids and Great Sphinxat Giza

Luxor is one of Egypt’s most famous ancient cities, often called the world’s greatest open-air museum. It sits on the east bank of the Nile, about 650 km south of Cairo, and was once the mighty capital of the New Kingdom pharaohs (then known as Thebes).

The Sinai Peninsula is a unique and striking region of Egypt, forming a triangular bridge between Africa and Asia. Bordered by the Mediterranean Sea to the north and the Red Sea to the south, it’s the only part of Egypt located in Asia, making it a crossroads of cultures, trade, and history.
